About us

Friends of the Ochils (FotO) is an independent Scottish charity (SC022034) committed to protecting the Ochils hills and their surrounding communities. Our aims are to:

  • Promote and conserve the landscape, natural beauty and wildlife of the Ochils, and protect the historical and social character of the surrounding communities.
  • Encourage consistent implementation of good planning policies appropriate to the Ochils.
  • Support responsible public access to the Ochils consistent with the Scottish Outdoor Access Code.
  • Promote appropriate study of and research into the Ochils and its local communities.

We are an independent voluntary association run by a committee of volunteers. Why not become a member and help us protect these wonderful hills?
