Our work

Friends of the Ochils campaigns to protect the Ochils from developments that threaten their landscape, wildlife, and recreational value. We respond to planning consultations, participate in public inquiries, and work with other organisations to preserve these hills for future generations. Browse our work below.

Windfarms

Our long-running campaign opposing inappropriate wind farm developments in and around the Ochils.

Beauly to Denny Power Line

The impact of the 400KV power line construction through the western Ochils and our response to it.

Forestry

Scrutinising forestry proposals to help preserve the upland character, beauty and natural capital of the Ochils.

Other planning issues

Other developments we have engaged with, including Glenquey Quarry and red squirrel conservation.

Newsletters

Our newsletters and annual reviews, keeping members up to date with campaigns and events.
