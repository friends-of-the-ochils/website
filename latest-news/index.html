Latest news

The latest news and updates from Friends of the Ochils — covering our campaigns, planning decisions, and conservation work in and around the Ochils.

  • FotO Responds to Confor Draft Engagement Guidelines

    1 March 2026

    FotO has submitted comments on Confor's draft guidelines for respectful and constructive engagement in Scottish forestry.

  • Balquhandy Forest Long Term Forest Plan — Consultation

    4 February 2026

    Scottish Forestry opened consultation on a 20-year management plan for Balquhandy Forest near Dunning. FotO reviewed the proposals ahead of the March 2026 deadline.

  • Craighead Windfarm Appeal Allowed — Eight Turbines Approved

    7 November 2025

    The Scottish Ministers' Reporter has allowed the planning appeal for the Craighead Windfarm near Dunning Glen, granting permission for eight wind turbines.

  • FotO Objects to Amended Brunt Hill Windfarm Application

    15 October 2025

    FotO has submitted a formal representation objecting to the amended Brunt Hill Windfarm application to the Energy Consents Unit.

  • FotO Raises Concerns Over Glenfarg Woodland Consultation

    22 September 2025

    FotO has written to Scottish Forestry expressing serious concerns about the late and inadequate stakeholder consultation on a new mixed commercial woodland proposal near Glenfarg.

  • Windburn Windfarm

    14 August 2025

    Friends of the Ochils have objected to the Windburn Windfarm development due to its significant adverse impact on the Ochils' landscape, visual amenity, and recreational use.

  • Blackford Farms Ochil Hills Woodland Creation — Stakeholder Engagement

    11 August 2025

    FotO is participating in stakeholder engagement for a proposed woodland creation scheme on the Blackford Farms estate in the Ochil Hills.

  • Ardargie Woodland Proposal: FotO Concludes Review

    3 May 2025

    FotO has concluded its formal review of the Ardargie Woodland Creation application, confirming no further concerns after key access issues were resolved.

  • Scotland's Rural Land Market and Natural Capital

  • Timber Harvesting — Dollar Forest 2021–2022

    An update on timber harvesting activity in Dollar Forest and what it means for the Ochils.

  • Saving Scotland's Red Squirrels
