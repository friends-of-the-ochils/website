---
title: FotO Objects to Amended Brunt Hill Windfarm Application
date: 2025-10-15
summary: FotO has submitted a formal representation objecting to the amended Brunt Hill Windfarm application to the Energy Consents Unit.
---

Friends of the Ochils has formally submitted its objection to the amended Brunt Hill Windfarm application to the Energy Consents Unit (ECU). The representation, reviewed and agreed by the FotO action team, was submitted on 15 October 2025 and has been officially acknowledged by the ECU.
