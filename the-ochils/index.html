About the hills

The Ochils are a range of hills in central Scotland, stretching from Stirling in the west to the Firth of Tay in the east. With 60 summits over 300 metres, they offer outstanding walking, wildlife and scenery within easy reach of much of Scotland's population. Explore the links below to learn more.
